# Art Direction

## Style

Painterly 2D digital game art for a romantic court intrigue game. The work should feel warm, elegant, readable, and slightly theatrical rather than photorealistic or vector-flat.

## Palette

Burgundy, deep teal, plum, cream parchment, antique gold, candle amber, and midnight blue. Route scenes should balance warm court light with cooler night shadows.

## Composition

Use clean silhouettes and one clear focal point. Backgrounds should support UI readability, with softer detail near the edges and no important content where overlays normally sit. Route panoramas should keep a readable central path.

## Current Asset Scope

The current simplified game only needs the hero background and three route backgrounds: Fast, Safe, and Secret. Character portraits, card illustrations, obstacles, and rival-trait art belong to older prototypes and should not be generated unless the game design brings them back.

## Avoid

No photorealism, captions, UI text, watermarks, logos, speech bubbles, overly modern clothing, inconsistent camera angles, harsh neon palettes, or cluttered backgrounds.
